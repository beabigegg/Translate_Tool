  behavior (AC-3 guarantee). No redeploy of other components required.
- **Full code rollback**: revert `app/backend/services/context_prompts.py` (remove `build_context_prefix`)
  and `app/backend/utils/translation_helpers.py` (remove prefix wire-in and `config` import). Delete
  `tests/test_context_window_segments.py`.
- **CI rollback**: no workflow additions were made; no workflow rollback needed.
